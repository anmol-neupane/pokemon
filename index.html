Type: Grass
Bulbasaur
Bulbasaur is a Grass/Poison-type Pokémon with a plant bulb on its back. It's known for being one of the original starter Pokémon in the Pokémon series. Calm and friendly, it evolves into Ivysaur and then Venusaur. As it grows, the bulb blooms into a large flower.

Pikachu
Pikachu is a yellow, mouse-like Pokémon known for its red cheeks and ability to generate electricity. It's the mascot of the Pokémon franchise and often seen with Ash Ketchum in the anime. Cute, friendly, and powerful, Pikachu evolves from Pichu and can evolve into Raichu.
